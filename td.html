Transportation Dashboard

Data Cleaning and Transformation

To create the dashboards, the following steps occurred:

  1. Data Cleaning:
    • Handling Missing Values: Ensured that all missing or null values in the dataset were replaced with appropriate defaults or interpolated.
    • Data Consistency: Standardized data formats for fields like dates, times, and categorical variables (e.g., gender, route names).
    • Removing Duplicates: Checked for duplicate entries and removed them to ensure accuracy in passenger counts and utilization metrics.
  2. Data Transformation:
    • Grouping and Aggregation:
      • Aggregated total riders by route, gender, age group, occupation, and time periods.
      • Summarized bus utilization rates and categorized them into "over-utilized," "under-utilized," and "well-utilized."
    • Time Analysis:
      • Extracted key time intervals (e.g., morning, afternoon, evening) and identified peak and off-peak hours.
      • Computed yearly and monthly passenger distributions.
    • Calculated Metrics:
      • Average riders per trip, total trips, and percentage contributions by categories (e.g., gender, age).
      • YoY (Year-over-Year) change for total passengers.
    • Categorization:
      • Classified buses based on performance (top-performing and underperforming).
      • Distinguished between AM and PM passenger distributions.

Key Insights (Summary)

  1. General Statistics:
    • Total Passengers: 6,587 passengers were recorded in the dataset.
    • Average Riders Per Trip: 33 riders per trip.
  2. Performance by Route:
    • Busiest Route: East-West Express with 1,322 riders.
    • Least Busy Route: South Line with 185 riders.
  3. Demographics:
    • Gender: Males account for the majority of riders (74%), followed by females (15%) and others (11%).
    • Age Group: The most represented age groups are 30–39 and 40–49, contributing significantly to ridership.
  4. Bus Utilization:
    • Peak Hour: 8:57 PM.
    • Off-Peak Hour: 7:50 PM.
    • Utilization Categories: 26% over-utilized, 49% well-utilized, and 25% under-utilized buses.
  5. Occupational Distribution:
    • The largest segment of riders includes "Other" (1,633) and "Professional" (1,554) groups.
  6. Yearly and Monthly Distribution:
    • Most ridership occurred in 2023 (5,654 passengers), with a sharp decline in 2024 (933 passengers).
    • December saw the highest monthly ridership.
  7. Weekly Patterns:
    • Weekdays, particularly Monday and Tuesday, saw the highest average ridership.
  8. Bus Performance:
    • Best Performing Bus: Bus 4.
    • Underperforming Buses: Bus 13.
  9. Rider Moments:
    • AM Riders: 35.4% of total passengers.
    • PM Riders: 64.6% of total passengers.
